The NU Fairview Student Executives (NUFV SE), the highest student representation and the central governing body of the students of NU Fairview. The central student government is for, by, and with the Nationalians. It inculcates transformative, dynamic, and progressive leadership for the student body. We, the NUFV SE, are the primary and foremost defender, protector, and promoter of the rights of the students.

Our tagline: “Leading through Service and Empowerment”

The third batch of Student Executives aims to create and expand opportunities for the students to enhance their wellbeing and for holistic growth, honing them as future nation builders.

Through dynamic, transformative, and progressive leadership, the central student government can achieve its mission this academic year for the Nationalian Community.

President Executive Orders

The NUFV SE President shall be responsible for cooperation, assistance, support, and engagement with all university offices and regional service organizations. The President serves as the head of the NUFV COP and consults with SE Officers on EOs for general NUFV SE activities.
