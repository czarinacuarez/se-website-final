THE UNIVERSITY WEEK FESTIVAL

About

University Week stands out as the highly anticipated annual event, serving as a platform to exhibit diverse talents and skills of both students and faculty members through various sports and activities.

This year, the NU Fairview Student Executives, in collaboration with the Student Development and Activities Office, are prepared to elevate the stature of U-Week 2024 under the theme ‘maNUfesting: Fated Victors.’

ma'NU'festing Fated Victors

Our visions portray Nationalians as individuals who not only excel but also choose to embody the spirit of champions. The distinguished week-long affair aims to unite students from all programs and schools in a series of meticulously curated sports tournaments and cultural activities, promoting teamwork, healthy competition, and a profound sense of belonging. 

This event will not merely emphasize the competitive aspect but also underscore our commitment to advancing the Sustainable Development Goals outlined by the United Nations.

Objectives

  • Cultivate Sportsmanship and Leadership: Cultivate an environment that fosters the development of sportsmanship and leadership qualities among participants, encouraging them to exhibit integrity, respect, and resilience both on and off the field.
  • Commemorate Tradition and Heritage: Commemorate the rich tradition and heritage of National University through a series of culturally significant events and activities, paying homage to our institution's core values and United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Unify the Student Body: Unify the diverse student body of NU Fairview by providing inclusive platforms for engagement, interaction, and collaboration, fostering a sense of unity, belonging, and pride among all participants.
